CYBERSCAPE

Instructions

- Move with WASD or Arrow Keys
- Left Click to shoot
- Press Shift to switch weapons
- Press T to place a Teleportation Hub (assign and enter ID to use)
- Press F to place a Data Mine (stores up to 10 shards)
- Press R to use Invisibility Shield (if purchased)
- Collect Keys to decrypt Data Shards at the Orange Central Hub
- Spend Shards in the Market for weapons, upgrades, and powerups
- Deliver decrypted shards to the Cyan Base Station to help AUREX recover
- Orange = Light Bots (fast, low HP)
- Red = Heavy Bots (slow, high HP)
- Purple = Sniper Bots (long range)
- Picking up a key would mean every bot on the map would lock in on you
- Teal = Safe Zone (bots disengage)
- Purple Tile = Bot Factory (spawns bots)
- Avoid or destroy bots and turrets
- Stay safe and outsmart enemy systems!
